What We Do

Research Focus

Skin Microbiome

Characterizing microbial community structures in healthy and diseased skin to identify key biomarkers.

Immune Mechanisms

Investigating how skin bacteria modulate immune cell development and antimicrobial responses.

Therapeutic Translation

Translating microbiome discoveries into novel treatments for skin conditions including acne and wound healing.
